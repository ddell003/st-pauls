Mission

Our mission is to support spiritual growth among men, helping men to mature as disciples as they encourage spiritual formation in others.

Goal

Our goal is to empower the ministry of Jesus Christ within our congregation and community. Men lead others while modeling Jesus Christ in thoughts, words, and deeds.
