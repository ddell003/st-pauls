Pre-School

St. Paul’s Little People Preschool offers, 2 year olds, 3 year olds and Pre-K children, a loving Christian learning environment that will encourage a desire to learn. We provide a developmentally appropriate program to allow and encourage your child’s cognitive, emotional, language, motor and social development. We also encourage, support and include parents in this exciting stage of your child’s development.

Serve

SERVEing at St. Paul’s is a way for both individuals and groups… teams, really… to plan, fund and work joyfully together to both take Christ’s love and care into our local community, and make the most of everyone’s giftsof time and treasure. We hope to make God’s love and Christ’s passion obvious to others in ways that can be seen, felt, tasted, experienced and known through works. We strive continually to live out the Scripture as found in Mark 10:45: “For the Son of Man came not to be served but to serve…” But what can I do with my gifts?
