WHAT IS CONNECT?

CONNECT IS PRAYER

praying for each other so that you may be healed. The prayer of a righteous person is powerful and effective. .James 5:16 Our prayer team recognizes the needs of individuals for encouragement and healing. This is a safe place where people can find trusting relationships and receive heart-felt prayer with Our Father.

CONNECT IS LEARNING

Jesus said we were to teach people to obey what He commanded. (Matthew 28:20) Connect groups are discussion-led and centered around learning how apply to the Word of God in our daily lives.

CONNECT is FELLOWSHIP

Therefore encourage one another and build one another up. 1 Thessalonians 5:11a. We are about relationships and want to help you have a genuine connection with God and other people. Fellowship activities are designed to help you meet new friends, get to know others, and build lifelong relationships with like-minded people. Connect has one simple fellowship purpose: to bring people together. We believe God created us to live in relationship with others and only then can we live the full life He intends for us.
